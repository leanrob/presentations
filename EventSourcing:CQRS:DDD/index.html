D.D.D. - Domain Driven Design

Domain Driven Design is the idea that a software system should match the domain that it is being built in.

  • Context
  • Domain
  • Model
  • Ubiquitous Language

C.Q.R.S. - Command Query Responsibility Separation

C.Q.R.S. is the concept that the command that a user sends to perform should be separated from the events that change the system.

  • Creates an audit log
  • Allows the system to be extendable even for past events
  • Scalability

Event Sourcing

Instead of keeping a record of only the current state of the data within a system, Event Sourcing keeps a record of the full series of actions taken on thats data

  • Append-only system
  • Events understandable for Domain Experts
  • Event Store

Event Sourcing

What is event sourcing?

Notes: These are notes Event sourcing is life. Or, that is, life is event-based. Everything in life at every level is an event that is stored in the event store of your life. At some point you held money in your hands and had a bank account, from then on your personal finances are just a series of events (some recorded and some not) that lead you up to your current financial situation. To take things deeper, people own psychies are event-driven, we are the person we are today because of the events and inputs our brain provided us up to this point. ----- But enough of the abstract, let's get more concrete. Event sourcing the idea that the state and data of a system is directly derived from a series of events, and that if those events were played back in the exact same order then they would provide an exact match in data and result. ----- ## Events, What are they and how to we get them? Events come from the real-world, domain experts who are knowledgable of the system that exists in the real-world will be used to extrpolate the events used in the system. The events of a system will ideally be an exact (or at least very close) representation of the real-world system.
